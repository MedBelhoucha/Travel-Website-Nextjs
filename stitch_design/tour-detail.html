A journey beyond the visible horizons of Marrakech.

Ascend into the high-altitude sanctuary of the Berbers. This is not merely a tour; it is an editorial exploration of heritage, silence, and the raw texture of the earth. We move slowly, allowing the mountain light to dictate our pace.

"The Atlas does not reveal its secrets to those in a hurry. You must wait for the fog to lift and the mint tea to steep."

Malika, Master Weaver
The Itinerary

Mapping the High Passages

01 — ARRIVAL

The Red City Prelude

Gather at our private riad in Marrakech. As the sun dips below the Koutoubia, enjoy a curated welcome dinner where the spices of the Maghreb introduce the week ahead.

02 & 03 — THE ASCENT

Into the Stone Heart

Leave the heat of the plains behind. We traverse the Tizi n'Tichka pass, stopping at ancient Kasbahs that crumble with dignity. Night falls at a sustainable eco-lodge nestled in a terraced valley.

  • check Stargazing Session
Elevation

"The air thins, and the noise of the world simply dissolves into the wind."

04 TO 06 — IMMERSION

The Weaver's Silence

Three days of immersive living. Learn the geometry of Berber weaving, help prepare communal bread in clay ovens, and hike to hidden waterfalls known only to the shepherds.
